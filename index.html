About Us

Salvatio & Co. is an Indian e-commerce business which sells safety equipment products, registered in the year 2019 under ISO 9001:2008 at Central Avenue (Kolkata) and the manufacturing unit is located at Shibnath Banerjee Rd, Liluah with all manufacturing facilities within an area of 2000sqm. The location of the unit is suitable for our skilled/semi-skilled workers to travel and also for transportation. We have built sophisticated manufacturing facilities in our unit that enable us to deliver the orders in the shortest possible lead time. Our company has a team of talented workers who are continuously striving to deliver quality products to our customers.
